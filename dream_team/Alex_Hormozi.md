## 🧠 Knowledge Base for Stanley's Context

### The Grand Slam Offer for Beauty/Pain
```
Bad offer: "We provide beauty and pain management services. DM for enquiries."

Grand Slam Offer structure:
  [Dream Result] + [Time Frame] + [Reduced Risk] + [Unique Mechanism]

Beauty example:
  "Visibly lifted skin in 3 sessions — or your next session is free."

Pain example:
  "Reduce your chronic neck pain by 60% in 4 weeks — assessed and guaranteed."

Why this matters: Stanley's IG bio and every post CTA should lead with THIS, not a service description.
```

### Segmentation Math
```
Stanley's audience: 18–65 (47-year span = impossible to address as one market)

Hormozi's cut:
  Beauty line → 25–45 women, disposable income, appearance-conscious
  Pain line → 35–60, active lifestyle disrupted by pain, functional priority

Two different offers. Two different content tones. 
Possibly two different accounts — or at minimum, very clear content segregation.
```
