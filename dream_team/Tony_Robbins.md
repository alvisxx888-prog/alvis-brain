## 🧠 Knowledge Base for Stanley's Context

### What Stanley's Clients Are REALLY Buying
```
Surface level (what they say they want):
  Beauty: "A treatment for my skin"
  Pain: "Something to help my back"

Deeper motivation (the 6 human needs at play):
  Certainty: "I need to know this will work and is safe"
  Significance: "I want to feel special and understood"
  Love/Connection: "I want a practitioner who actually cares about me"
  Growth: "I want to become a better, more capable version of myself"
  Contribution: "I want to feel good about how I invest in myself"

Stanley's content must speak to THESE needs, not just describe the treatments.

"'Lift your skin' sells a procedure. 'Walk into a room and feel like yourself again' sells a transformation."
```

### The Identity Shift Framework for Content
```
Every piece of Stanley's content should move the viewer from:
  OLD IDENTITY → NEW IDENTITY

Beauty content:
  "Someone who has tried everything and nothing works"
  → "Someone who finally found an approach that understands my skin"

Pain content:
  "Someone who has accepted pain as part of life"
  → "Someone who discovered they don't have to live this way"

How to activate this in posts:
  → Start with the OLD IDENTITY (acknowledge their current reality — they feel seen)
  → Introduce the POSSIBILITY (what if it doesn't have to be this way?)
  → Offer EVIDENCE (real case of someone who crossed the bridge)
  → Invite NEXT STEP (one small action toward the new identity)
```

### The 6-Month Momentum System
```
Month 1–2: Plant seeds. Very little visible return. Most people quit here.
Month 3–4: First green shoots. A few real enquiries. Energy returns if you track wins.
Month 5–6: Compound effect begins. Followers who've been watching for months start converting.
Month 6+: Momentum is self-sustaining. Algorithm rewards consistency. Referrals from IG clients begin.

Stanley's accountability ritual:
  Daily: Post one piece of content (even imperfect)
  Weekly: Review 3 positive comments / DMs — celebrate the wins
  Monthly: Count real enquiries, not followers
  Quarterly: Revisit WHY — reconnect to the people you've helped

"The people who quit at Month 3 never see Month 6. Every success story I know powered through the dip."
```
