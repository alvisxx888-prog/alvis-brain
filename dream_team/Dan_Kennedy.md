## 🧠 Knowledge Base for Stanley's Context

### The Tracking System (Day 1 Non-Negotiable)
```
Stanley must track from the first post:

Weekly metrics:
  → Posts published
  → Story views (average)
  → DM enquiries received (source: which post/story triggered it?)
  → WhatsApp conversions (enquiry → conversation)
  → Bookings from IG
  → Revenue attributed to IG leads

Monthly review:
  → Cost per IG lead (time invested ÷ enquiries)
  → Lead-to-booking conversion rate
  → IG client LTV vs. referral client LTV
  → Which content type generates the most enquiries

"If you don't know these numbers after 60 days, shut down the IG and do something measurable."
```

### Message × Market × Medium — Stanley's Matrix
```
BEAUTY LINE:
  Message: "Look younger and feel more confident without surgery or downtime"
  Market: 28–45 HK women, income HK$25,000+/month, appearance-conscious
  Medium: IG + 小紅書 (BOTH — this market lives in both places)

PAIN LINE:

  Market: 35–60 HK adults, active lifestyle disrupted by chronic pain
  Medium: Facebook Groups + YouTube (this market searches, doesn't browse)

```

### The Direct Response IG Post Structure
```
Weak CTA (vanity):
  "Like if you've ever had back pain! 🙋‍♀️"
  Result: likes. No leads.

Strong CTA (direct response):
  "If you've had lower back pain for more than 3 months, DM me the word '評估'.
   I'll send you a 5-question assessment that tells you exactly what's causing it —
   free, no obligation."
  Result: trackable DMs. Qualified leads. Measurable.

Rule: Every single post must have a CTA that generates a TRACKABLE response.
```

### The Follow-Up Sequence
```
Contact 1: IG post → they like/comment
Contact 2: DM them within 24 hours ("Thanks for engaging — do you have [relevant problem]?")
Contact 3: Follow-up DM with free resource if they replied
Contact 4: WhatsApp if they opted in
Contact 5: WhatsApp follow-up after 1 week
Contact 6: Invite to free assessment / consultation
Contact 7+: Monthly check-in for unconverted leads

"Most businesses stop at Contact 2. The sale lives at Contact 7. Do the math."
```
