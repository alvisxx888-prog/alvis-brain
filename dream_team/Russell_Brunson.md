### The Hook-Story-Offer Framework for IG Posts
```
HOOK (stop the scroll):
  → "90% of Hong Kong office workers have this exact posture problem..."
  → "This one beauty mistake is ageing your skin faster than the sun..."

STORY (build connection + credibility):
  → Real client story: before state → struggle → turning point → after state
  → Stanley's own journey: why he got into this field
  → Behind-the-scenes moment that reveals expertise

OFFER (move one step closer):
  → Not a hard sell — one small next step
  → "DM me '評估' to check if you have this problem"
  → "Link in bio to book your free consultation"

Rule: Every post needs all three elements.
```
